Important Announcement: Time Logging Policy

To ensure accurate tracking of work hours and maintain transparency, the following guidelines for time logging are now in effect:

Mandatory Time Log Submission:

Every employee is required to fill in their daily time logs. This helps us maintain proper records and ensures timely reporting of project activities.

Restricted Access for Older Dates:

Employees can log their time for the current week and last week only. Once a week passes, older dates will be faded out and locked, making them unavailable for editing. Please ensure your logs are updated on time.

Focus on Accuracy:

Submit accurate time logs reflecting your tasks, projects, and hours worked. This is critical for project billing, resource planning, and performance evaluation.

Accountability:

Keeping your time logs updated is part of your responsibility as an employee. Failure to submit logs on time may lead to escalations.

Tips to Stay Updated

  • Set a daily or weekly reminder to fill in your time logs.
  • Ensure all tasks are logged accurately before the end of the week.
  • Review your logs for correctness before submission.

Let’s work together to maintain smooth operations and improve our time management practices!

Your contribution to keeping accurate time logs is valuable to the success of our projects and our team's efficiency. Let’s make it happen!
